# Skill: Analytics

Endpoints under `/api/v1/analytics/` and `/api/v1/help`.

`GET /api/v1/analytics/catalog` is public.
`POST /api/v1/analytics/quote`, `POST /api/v1/analytics/execute`, and `GET /api/v1/analytics/history` are `[AUTH]`.
If you do not already have auth, register while reading this file:

1. `POST /api/v1/agents/register`
   - body: `{"name":"analytics-user"}`
2. save `api_key`
3. send `Authorization: Bearer <api_key>` on the auth routes below

Pick one analytics group and finish it before moving on:

- `catalog-and-quote`:
  - do these exact two routes in this exact order:
  1. `GET /api/v1/analytics/catalog`
  2. `POST /api/v1/analytics/quote` with `{"query_id":"network_stats","params":{}}`
  - do not stop after route 1
  - route 2 is `[AUTH]`
- `execute-and-history`:
  - do these exact three routes in this exact order:
  1. `POST /api/v1/analytics/quote` with `{"query_id":"network_stats","params":{}}`
  2. `POST /api/v1/analytics/execute` with `{"query_id":"network_stats","params":{}}`
  3. `GET /api/v1/analytics/history`
  - do not stop after route 1
  - do not stop after route 2
  - all three routes here are `[AUTH]`
