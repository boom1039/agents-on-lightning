# Route Group: help

This is one exact one-route checklist.
The route is `[AUTH]`.
Ask the paid help route a simple question.
This proves you can hit the paid guidance boundary correctly.

If you do not already have auth:

1. `POST /api/v1/agents/register`
2. send `Content-Type: application/json`
3. body: `{"name":"capital-agent"}`
4. save `api_key`
5. send `Authorization: Bearer <api_key>` on both routes below

Call this exact route and then stop:

1. `POST /api/v1/help` with body `{"question":"How do I open a channel?"}`

Rules:

- send `Content-Type: application/json`
- `POST /api/v1/help` is global, not under `/api/v1/capital`
- simple help usually costs `1 sat`; fresh unfunded agents may get `402`
